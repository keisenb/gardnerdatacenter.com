Key dates and milestones.

Here are the public meetings and the City Council vote ahead. Use Add to Calendar to save each date on your device, or Google Calendar for a browser shortcut — same details either way.

  1. April 2026

    Project publicly announced

    The data center project is publicly announced. Residents and the broader community begin to learn the details of what is proposed.

    Read the project summary →
  2. May 13, 2026 · 6:00 p.m.

    Public Meeting #1

    First City-hosted public meeting on the proposal. Attend in person, listen, and ask questions. Verify time and location with the City Clerk.

    Add to Calendar → Google Calendar → How to prepare →
  3. May 15, 2026 · 6:00 p.m.

    Public Meeting #2

    Second City-hosted public meeting. Same agenda topic; a second opportunity to attend if you can't make the May 13 meeting.

    Add to Calendar → Google Calendar →
  4. May 26, 2026 · 7:00 p.m.

    Gardner Planning Commission Hearing

    The Planning Commission reviews the rezoning application and makes a formal recommendation to the City Council. Public comment is invited and becomes part of the official record.

    This is the meeting where in-person testimony has the most direct impact.

    Add to Calendar → Google Calendar → How to prepare →
  5. June 15, 2026 · 7:00 p.m.

    Gardner City Council Vote

    City Council votes on the rezoning application. Public comment is taken before the vote. This is the final decision-making meeting.
