## Content inspiration

The information architecture for this site (water, power, noise, air, tax
abatements, precedent, data center bubble) was inspired by the work of the
Spring Hill, Kansas neighbors at
[springhilldatacenter.com](https://springhilldatacenter.com/). All copy on
this site was written from scratch for the Gardner community context using
publicly available reporting from KCUR, the Texas Observer, the Wichita
Eagle, the U.S. Department of Energy / LBNL Center of Expertise for Data
Center Efficiency, and other public sources.
