Johnson County

If you live in unincorporated Johnson County, your direct elected representative is your County Commissioner.

The Johnson County Board of Commissioners does not vote on Gardner rezoning. But your County Commissioner represents you on county-level land use, regional planning, infrastructure, and emergency-service capacity — and can speak publicly on impacts to unincorporated residents. The 6th District covers much of southern Johnson County, including the Gardner / Edgerton area.

Email template

A short, respectful email — copy, edit, send.

Officials read the most weight into emails that are clearly from real residents in their own words. Use the template below as a starting point — change a sentence or two so it sounds like you, and add your own reason for being concerned.

Subject: Please oppose industrial-scale data center rezoning in Gardner

To: the Mayor, all City Council members, and the City Clerk

Dear Mayor Winters and Members of the City Council,

I'm a resident of Gardner and I'm writing to ask you to oppose any rezoning that would allow an industrial-scale (hyperscale) data center near our community.

I share these concerns:

  1. Water — A typical 100 MW data center uses around 530,000 gallons of water per day, comparable to about 6,500 homes. Our local water systems should not be put at risk to serve a private industrial campus.

  2. Power and utility costs — Massive new electrical demand often means new substations, transmission upgrades, and higher rates that local households end up paying for.

  3. Noise and air quality — Continuous cooling fans and regular diesel generator testing affect nearby homes, schools, and farms. PM2.5 emissions are particularly harmful to children.

  4. M-1 zoning is permanent — Even if this specific developer never builds, the zoning change would remain and would set a precedent for further industrial proposals.

  5. Process — Residents deserve a clear timeline, full technical documents, and a real opportunity for public review before any vote.

I'm not anti-development. I support thoughtful, planned growth for Gardner. But heavy industry next to homes, schools, and farms is the wrong fit for our community.

Please vote no on any rezoning that would enable this kind of project.

Thank you for your time and your service to our community.

Sincerely,
[Your name]
[Your street address, Gardner, KS]
[Phone number — optional]

Even better: change a few sentences to reflect your own reasons. A unique, personal email lands harder than a hundred copy-pastes.
